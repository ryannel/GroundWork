# Distillation Plan — Principles Corpus

Status: PROPOSED (input to the master distillation plan)
Scope: `src/docs/principles/` — foundations/ 16,672 w · system-design/ 16,313 w · design/ 9,759 w · quality/ 9,211 w · delivery/ 8,181 w · ai-native/ 7,417 w · stack/ 1,980 w · index.md 699 w — plus `src/docs/ways-of-working/` 2,159 w and `src/docs/llms.txt`. Total ≈ 72,391 words (`wc -w`).

## 1. Intention

### Who actually reads this corpus

The corpus has three real consumer classes, verified against the tree:

1. **Tier-2 seeded docs in user projects.** `src/docs/` ships to `docs/` at init (contributor guide tier table: hash-classified on `update` — pristine auto-refresh, edited → merge queue). A human on the team and any project agent can read it in situ; `src/docs/llms.txt` is the routing index that points agents at every file. `groundwork-check` deliberately exempts `docs/principles/**` and `docs/ways-of-working/**` from code-drift checks (12-month `last_reviewed` advisory only) — so nothing mechanical catches staleness here except the review date.
2. **Distillation source for the discipline personas and engineer skills.** Eight sync-anchors pin corpus files by SHA-256 (`scripts/check_sync_anchors.py`, gated in `./dev test contracts`): `groundwork-architect` (21 principle files + `index.md`), `groundwork-product` (7), `groundwork-designer` (8), and the five engineer skills (5–7 each, all drawing on the same cross-cutting canon: `foundations/testing.md` ×5, `foundations/documentation.md` ×5, `foundations/code-craft.md` ×1, `quality/observability.md` ×6, `quality/security.md` ×6, `quality/reliability.md` ×5, `quality/performance.md` ×5, `quality/accessibility.md` ×3). Every edit to a pinned file forces a same-commit review of the matching persona/engineer reference.
3. **Runtime path references from shipped skills.** The bet skill loads corpus files *in the user project* by path: `docs/principles/foundations/testing.md` is cited in `slice-worker.md`, `coverage-auditor.md`, `acceptance-auditor.md`, and `templates/bet-progress-test.md`; `docs/principles/delivery/day-2-operational-baseline.md` is invoked by `groundwork-scaffold`, `groundwork-stack-forge`, and bet `04-delivery.md`. `groundwork-check` reads `ways-of-working` doc types. These files are load-bearing infrastructure, not just reading material.

So the corpus's reason to exist: **hold the stances — with the reasoning that makes them defensible — that (a) the persona/engineer distillers compress into decision-time references, (b) shipped skills invoke as the named bar during delivery, and (c) a project team inherits as its seeded engineering culture.** A file earns its slot by carrying a stance a consumer acts on. Exposition a reader could get from any textbook is cut material; process truth that belongs to the operating contract or the bet skill is re-homed material.

### Per-subdirectory intention map

**`index.md`** — the manifesto: nine beliefs every other page hangs off. Pinned by the architect.

**`foundations/`** — the product-and-engineering spine (product persona distils six of these; engineer skills distil testing/documentation/code-craft):
- `product-engineering.md` — outcomes over outputs; shaped work; appetite; instrument what ships. The product-corpus spine page.
- `continuous-discovery.md` — discovery as habit; opportunity-solution tree; vision leads.
- `product-risks.md` — the four risks, owners, riskiest-assumption-first.
- `prioritization-and-appetite.md` — portfolio sizing on worth + stakes; effort deflated by AI.
- `success-metrics.md` — decide the signal before shipping; North Star; counter-metrics.
- `requirements-and-specs.md` — jobs-to-be-done; stable IDs; testable acceptance criteria.
- `code-craft.md` — simplicity, no speculative abstraction, deletion, boundary parsing.
- `testing.md` — honeycomb + independent oracles + mutation signal + front-door proof; the runtime-cited bar for bet delivery.
- `documentation.md` — docs as canonical knowledge / skills as execution layer; Diátaxis; generated reference; freshness model.

**`system-design/`** — the architect's decision references (all ten pinned by the architect): code-structure (pure core / swappable edges — the archetype), api-design, architecture-decisions, evolutionary-architecture, integration-patterns, durable-execution, data-engineering, identity-and-access, real-time, surface-architecture.

**`quality/`** — the cross-cutting quality bars, the most-pinned files in the corpus (observability/security ×6 anchors each): reliability, observability, performance, security, privacy, accessibility.

**`delivery/`** — how software reaches and survives production: progressive-delivery, platform, devex, cost-engineering (all pinned by the architect except devex and day-2), plus `day-2-operational-baseline.md` — the stack-agnostic operational bar shipped skills invoke by path.

**`ai-native/`** — the probabilistic-core overlays: ai-engineering (prompts/evals/context/RAG), agentic-systems (agent-as-actor architecture), agent-native-systems (interfaces agents consume), ai-native-product (product management over a model). All pinned by the architect; ai-native-product also by the product persona.

**`design/`** — the designer's corpus (all seven pinned by the designer): design-foundations (spine), visual-design, layout-and-space, interaction-and-motion, usability-and-ux, design-systems-and-tokens, ai-native-design.

**`stack/`** — one file, `postgres.md`: the default-datastore stance. No sync-anchor pins it, no skill references it; the per-stack idiom canon that the contributor guide describes lives with the generators (`src/generators/go-microservice/docs/principles/stack/go/…`), not here.

**`ways-of-working/`** — the user-facing statement of how a GroundWork project runs: `how-we-work.md` (lifecycle), `units-of-work.md` (bet/milestone/slice vocabulary), `documentation.md` (living documents, doc hierarchy, ADR mechanics). Referenced by `groundwork-check`; indexed by llms.txt; not pinned by any anchor.

### The honest headline

The 2026-06-19 fresh-slate rewrite produced a corpus that is, file by file, in strong shape: nearly every page is genuinely stance-first, carries decision rules rather than surveys, names its counter-position honestly, and cites sources. The distillation problem here is **not** widespread textbook bloat. It is four specific things: (1) `ways-of-working/` was never touched by the rewrite (`last_reviewed: 2026-05-26`) and now states process truth the bet skill has overturned three times since; (2) a handful of cross-file duplications where the same doctrine has two or three full homes; (3) a few reference-book pockets (vendor catalogs, browser-support trivia) that will rot fastest and carry the least stance; (4) two orphaned/unpinned files whose consumption story is unresolved. The plan below is therefore surgical, and it is sequenced around the sync-anchor blast radius, because that — not the word count — is the expensive dimension of editing this section.
